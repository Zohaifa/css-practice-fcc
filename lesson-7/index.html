Three types of Martial Arts

In the world of combat sports and martial arts, fighting can generally be broken down into three main components:
1. striking
2. grappling, and
3. wrestling.
Each of these elements plays a crucial role in determining the outcome of a fight, and martial artists often specialize in one or more of them to create a well-rounded skill set. Striking focuses on delivering powerful and precise blows from a distance, grappling involves controlling and submitting an opponent through holds and locks, while wrestling revolves around positioning and dominating the opponent through takedowns and control. Whether in a traditional martial art or in mixed martial arts (MMA), mastering these three aspects allows a fighter to adapt to different situations, creating a balanced and versatile approach to combat.
